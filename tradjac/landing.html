Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Innovation

Since 2009, TradJac has evolved from a regional logistics provider into a trusted 3PL partner for businesses across India.

The foundation of TradJac's cold storage facility was shaped by the vision of Mr. Badruddin Faiyaz Valikarimwala and Yogesh Rathod. Their dedication and leadership helped create a modern cold chain facility focused on reliability, product safety, and operational excellence.
