About TradJac

TradJac is a trusted 3PL logistics partner delivering advanced warehousing, cold chain, and supply chain solutions. We specialize in handling temperature-sensitive and dry goods with precision, ensuring product integrity, safety, and efficiency at every stage — from storage to final delivery.

With strategically located facilities, modern infrastructure, and technology-driven operations, we empower businesses to scale faster, reduce costs, and maintain consistent quality across their supply chain.

Our Core Services

Temperature Controlled Warehousing

Advanced cold storage with multi-temperature zones (-25°C to +25°C), real-time monitoring, pre-cooling areas, and 100% power backup. Designed for perishables like fruits, dairy, seafood, and pharmaceuticals.

Dry & Ambient Warehousing

Flexible storage solutions with high-capacity racking, WMS tracking, humidity control, and secure infrastructure. Ideal for FMCG, retail, e-commerce, and industrial goods.

Value Added Services

End-to-end support including blast freezing, packing, sorting, labeling, order fulfillment, and reverse logistics — helping you reduce costs and improve delivery speed.
